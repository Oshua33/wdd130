Live Adventures

Urubamba River Classic

Float past ancient Incan terraces and lush cloud forest on Peru's Sacred Valley river. Perfect for newbies and families. This half-day trip covers 12km of beautiful Class II/III rapids.

Class II/III Half Day Beginner Friendly

Colca Canyon Expedition

One of South America's most dramatic river canyons — twice as deep as the Grand Canyon. This full-day multi-sport excursion combines white water rafting, canyon hiking, and condor watching.

Class III/IV Full Day Intermediate

Amazon Headwaters Extreme

For thrill-seekers only. Navigate the wild upper tributaries of the Amazon through dense jungle and thundering Class V rapids. A 3-day expedition with riverside camping — the ultimate Peruvian adventure.

Class IV/V 3 Days Expert Only
