Why Raft With Us?

Expert Guides

Our guides have over 40 years of combined experience navigating Peru's most challenging rivers. Safety is our top priority — your adventure is in expert hands every step of the way.

Premium Equipment

We provide top-of-the-line gear including helmets, life jackets, and modern rafts. All equipment is inspected before every trip so you can focus entirely on the thrill of the river.

Breathtaking Scenery

Paddle through ancient canyons, past Incan ruins, and through lush Amazon rainforest. Every trip is as visually stunning as it is heart-pumping — Peru's rivers are unlike anywhere else.

All Skill Levels Welcome

Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, we have a trip designed for you. From gentle Class II floats to roaring Class V rapids — choose your level of adventure.
